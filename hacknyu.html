Overview

Designed the full visual identity for HackNYU, with Google as one of the sponsors, hackathon with 600+ participants. Using Figma, Illustrator, Procreate and Photoshop, I crafted vibrant t-shirts, stickers, signage, and digital assets that captured the event's energy and theme.

I collaborated with cross-functional teams to ensure consistent branding across social media, registration, and on-site experience. The final designs elevated the event's presence, boosted attendee engagement, and strengthened HackNYU's brand recognition within the tech community.
