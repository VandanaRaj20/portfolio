Illustrations

In between projects and deadlines, I find space to draw—capturing moods, stories, and abstract ideas through illustration. This collection features original artworks created using Procreate and Illustrator, each a reflection of my personal creative voice and curiosity.

From whimsical characters to serene landscapes, these illustrations are where I explore narrative, form, and emotion—fundamental principles I also bring into my UX design work.
